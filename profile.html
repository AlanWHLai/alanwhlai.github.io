Introduction

歡迎來到我的個人網頁，這個網頁希望能協助你認識我是怎麼樣的人。 在這裡，我會分享過去一段時間的經歷、我（有時候和其他人合作）寫的文章或在各個角落的努力成果，以及其他我有幸能夠知道、想分享的事物。
此外，這個網頁本身是我為了學習網頁製作做的練習。 我希望未來能持續進步，把網站改的更完整和漂亮。 雖然如果請AI協助或直接使用模板的話，或許可以更簡單地完成，且在實際上品質更好的網站。 但在學習過程中我喜歡走得慢一點、繞點遠路，我相信這樣能讓我得到些額外的收穫和成長。或至少，學會新東西讓我有成就感。

Welcome to my personal webpage. This site aims to help you get to know what kind of person I am. Here, I will share the journey I have been through, articles I (sometimes co-work with friends and others) have written, other projects I have done (or at least participated and contributed), as well as anything that I am lucky enough to know and want to share.
This website itself is a practice for learning web design and programming. I hope to continue improving and make the site more complete and beautiful in the future. While using AI assistance or templates might allow for simpler completion and higher quality website in practical terms, I prefer to take it slow during my learning process. I believe that this approach will give me some additional gains and growth. At least, getting things clear gives me a sense of achievement.
